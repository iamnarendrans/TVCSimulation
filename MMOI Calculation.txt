MMOI - Mass Moment of Intertia
Example
Mass: 0.543kg
COM-String: 0.3m
String Len: 0.65m
COM-TVC: 0.28m
Rotation Sample Ts: 16.03s for 10 osciallation
Ts = 16.03/10 = 1.603

MMOI = Mass * g * Ts^2 * COM-String^s / 4*pi^2*String Len
MMOI = (0.543 * 9.81 * 1.603^2 * 0.3^2) / ( 4*pi^2*0.65)
MMOI = 0.048 Kgm^2
Moment Arm = 0.28
